Unlike dogs, cats do not have a sweet tooth. Scientists believe this is due to a mutation in a key taste receptor.
When a cat chases its prey, it keeps its head level. Dogs and humans bob their heads up and down.
The technical term for  cats hairball is a bezoar.
A group of cats is called a clowder.
A cat cannot climb head first down a tree because every claw on a their paw points the same way. To get down from a tree, a cat must back down.
A cat can travel at a top speed of approximately 49 km over a short distance.
